Basil Technologies

Welcome to Basil Technologies – empowering nonprofits to shape a sustainable future.

Meet Basil

We are a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization that provides pro-bono consulting to influential organizations pioneering a healthier and more environmentally sustainable California. Our consultants are high achieving outliers from a broad range of industries and academic disciplines with an insatiable desire to serve.

Our Team

The Basil team is comprised of developers, designers, and product managers from the world's leading technology companies.

What We Do

Mobile Apps

We leverage the best in mobile technology, including React Native and more to deliver impactful apps to those on the ground.

Websites

We help non-profits with branding, web development and marketing so that they can tell their message in the most impactful way.

Consulting

Many people are doing impactful and meaningful work accross the globe but have no idea what role technology can play, we can help with that.

Hackathons

Have a huge problem you’re trying to solve? We can organize hackathons in your area of work to mobilize the industry's brightest to deliver prototypes in under a week.
